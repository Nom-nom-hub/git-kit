## Outline

2. **Path**: Target `.github/CHARTER.md`.
3. **Content**:

4. **Update**: Merge user's specific project rules into the template.

## Guidelines
- The charter is the "Law" of the repo.
- Be clear and enforceable.
- Reference industry standards (e.g., Airbnb style guide) if helpful.
